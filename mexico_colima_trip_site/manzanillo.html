Best base areas to research

For a first group trip, research the central tourist/port zones, historic center and Malecón, the hotel zone, Península Santiago / Las Hadas, and Club Santiago-style areas. Keep routes simple and avoid unplanned late-night exploring.

Beach-day flow

Morning: leave early, breakfast, beach chairs/palapa.
Midday: swim, water activity, seafood.
Evening: Malecón, sunset, dinner, back before it gets too late.

Group essentials

Bring cash pesos, towels, waterproof phone pouch, sunglasses, sunscreen, IDs, portable charger, and a planned ride back before everyone gets tired.

Photo ideas

White outfits by the beach, papel-picado color accents, sunset family photo, palm-tree walking shot, seafood table shot, and a “Colima 2027” group pose.
